About

Ed is a London-based actor with an MA in acting from East 15. He appeared in a number of notable productions throughout his course, including playing Slim in Sam Shepard's "Cowboy Mouth" and Gayev in "The Cherry Orchard." Ed also devised and performed in the sell-out comedy piece "SW1A 1AA" at the Clapham Fringe festival.

Before pursuing a career in acting, Ed worked as a software developer and earned a master's degree in mechanical engineering. In addition to his acting training and experience, he has a number of skills and specialties, including singing, voice over, various sports, and a good ear for accents. He is always seeking new and challenging roles and is excited to continue building a successful career in the acting industry.
